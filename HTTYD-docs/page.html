Founded on February 24, 2009. We currently have 6,271 articles, 110,059 files, 1,134,146 dragons trained, and 26 dragon trainers.

Human Character


Atali is the leader of the Wingmaidens and the caretaker of Wingnut. She has sworn an oath to protect vulnerable baby Razorwhips and now resides on Wingmaiden Island, where she and her tribe raise the hatchlings until they can take care of themselves.
Learn more...

Dragon Character


Hiccup's Windwalker is a male Windwalker and Hiccup's riding dragon. Despite having a limping foreleg, he is one of the fastest dragons in the archipelago. His speed is only matched by his great loyalty towards Hiccup, fighting alongside humans in the Dragon War.
Learn more...

Species


The Flyhopper is a small flightless dragon that lives in large packs. Despite lacking fire and wings, Flyhoppers are capable of gliding for short distances and defending themselves by using their shovel-like tail to throw objects at their attackers.
Learn more...

Other


Wendy is an Artificial Intelligence owned by Alexandra Gonzalez. Having a soft feminine voice, Wendy is one of Alex's closest friends, even if it is not a real person. It is also an advanced piece of technology, capable of doing various complex tasks, such as playing chess.
Learn more...
